Description of Target Audience

The target audience for this site is small business owners and individuals who are interested in community development.

Personas

  • John: A local farmer who needs to make a profit wants to be informed about local farmers market permits and information to sell his products.
  • Cori: Wants to stay on top of PTA meetings and be involved in the community.

Scenarios

  • When will the next town fair be held?
  • What are the dates school starts?
  • How can I register my business for a permit at the farmers market?
